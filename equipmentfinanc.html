What is Equipment Financing?

Equipment Financing is a type of small business loan designed specifically to purchase business-related machinery, vehicles, or equipment. In this structure, the equipment itself usually serves as the collateral for the loan, making it easier to qualify for and protecting your personal assets.

How it works:

  • The lender provides the funds needed to purchase the specific equipment.
  • You make regular, fixed monthly payments over a predetermined term.
  • The equipment acts as collateral; once the loan is fully paid, you own the equipment free and clear.

Key Benefits:

  • Preserves Working Capital – Get the expensive machinery you need without depleting your daily cash reserves.
  • Self-Collateralizing – Because the equipment secures the loan, you typically don't need to pledge additional assets or real estate.
  • Tax Advantages – Equipment purchases often qualify for tax deductions (such as Section 179), reducing your overall tax burden.

Qualifying Factors:

  • A solid credit profile (business and personal).
  • The specific type, condition, and value of the equipment being purchased.
  • Proof of business revenue and time in business.

Which Businesses Are Ideal for This Type of Financing?

Equipment Financing is perfect for businesses that rely heavily on specialized machinery, vehicles, or technology to operate and generate revenue. It allows you to upgrade outdated tools or expand your capabilities without taking a massive hit to your cash flow.

Ideal for Businesses In Industries Such As:

  • Construction & Heavy Contracting
  • Transportation & Trucking
  • Manufacturing & Warehousing
  • Medical & Dental Practices
  • Restaurants & Food Service

Benefits:

  • Quickly upgrade to the latest technology to stay competitive.
  • Spread the cost of expensive, long-lasting assets over their useful life.
  • Flexible terms that can be tailored to the expected lifespan of the equipment.
